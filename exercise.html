Types of Workouts

Strength Training

Build muscle, increase metabolism, and improve endurance using weights or bodyweight exercises.

Cardio Training

Improve heart health with running, cycling, dancing, or high-intensity interval training.

Flexibility & Mobility

Enhance movement and reduce injury through stretching and activities like yoga or Pilates.
